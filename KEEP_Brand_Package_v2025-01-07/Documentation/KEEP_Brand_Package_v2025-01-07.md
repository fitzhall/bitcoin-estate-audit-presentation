## Brand Guidelines

### Color Specifications

**Primary Palette**
- Deep Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (Primary dark)
- Off-White: `#F9F9F9` (Primary light)

**Usage Rules**
- Maximum 2 colors per application
- High contrast required for all applications
- Maintain 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum

### Typography

**Wordmark Specifications**
- Custom sans-serif, all caps "KEEP"
- Precision optical kerning
- "Invisible grid" perfection
- Enhanced letter spacing for premium feel

### Logo Construction

**The Keystone Symbol**
- Geometric architectural form
- Vertical stroke as central structural support
- Diagonal strokes create foundational keystone shape
- Mathematical precision in all angles
- Optimized negative space
