### Content Quality Framework

**Minimum Content Standards**:
- 2,000+ words per page minimum
- 10+ unique data points per page
- 5+ internal links to related content
- 3+ external authoritative sources
- Professional-grade writing and editing

**SEO Optimization Standards**:
- Target keyword in title, H1, and first paragraph
- 2-3% keyword density throughout content
- Semantic keyword variations and related terms
- Meta description and title tag optimization
- Schema markup for enhanced search visibility

**User Experience Standards**:
- Clear navigation and content structure
- Mobile-responsive design and formatting
- Fast loading times and performance optimization
- Accessible design and content formatting
- Clear calls-to-action and conversion optimization
